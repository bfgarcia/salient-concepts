Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 27, 2026

Overview

Salient Concepts LLC ("we", "us") operates the Aria home automation assistant. This policy describes how we handle information related to our SMS notification service.

Information We Collect

We collect and store phone numbers of household members who have been manually registered by the account administrator with their explicit consent. We also retain message logs for service reliability purposes.

How We Use Your Information

Phone numbers are used solely to deliver SMS notifications requested by household members via voice command. Notifications include timer alerts, home automation status updates, and informational responses.

Information Sharing

Your mobile information will not be sold or shared with third parties for promotional or marketing purposes. We do not share personal information with any third parties except as required to deliver messages (i.e., our messaging carrier) or as required by law.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on user requests. Messages are only sent when explicitly triggered by a registered household member.

Opt-Out

You may opt out of SMS notifications at any time by replying STOP to any message. You may also request removal of your phone number by contacting us.

Data Security

Phone numbers and message data are stored on privately hosted, encrypted infrastructure within our local network. No data is stored on public cloud services.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact us at sc01@salient-concepts.com.
