Aria Home Assistant

About Us

Salient Concepts LLC builds privacy-focused smart home technology. Our flagship product, Aria, is an AI-powered home assistant that runs on locally hosted infrastructure — your data never leaves your network.

Aria integrates with home automation systems to provide voice-controlled lighting, climate, security, and more. She also delivers timely notifications via SMS to registered household members.

What Aria Does

Voice Control

Natural language commands for lights, thermostats, locks, and other smart home devices.

Notifications

SMS alerts for timers, reminders, and home automation events — only when you ask.

Private by Design

All processing runs on local hardware. No cloud storage of conversations or personal data.

Household Aware

Recognizes household members by voice and face for personalized responses.

SMS Notification Service

Aria can send text messages to registered household members for timer alerts, status updates, and informational responses. Messages are sent only when explicitly requested by a registered user via voice command.

Consent: SMS recipients are manually registered by the household administrator with their verbal consent. Consent is not a condition of any purchase.

Frequency: Message frequency varies based on user requests. Msg & data rates may apply.

Opt-out: Reply STOP to any message to unsubscribe. Reply START to resubscribe. Reply HELP for assistance.

Privacy: Your mobile information will not be sold or shared with third parties for promotional or marketing purposes.
